Creativity is our DNA

I'm Angelo! Owner of LinFre Digital. Making music, drawing and DJing are just some of my creative endevors. With a curiousity of how video games are made, I found programming and in 2020, I finally decided to be my own boss!

Having worked with a diverse range of businesses for 3 years, I discovered a recurring pattern among small businesses: they either don't have the budget to invest in a high-quality website, or if they do, they get a website that is slow, clunky and often looks outdated. As a result, they find themselves in a difficult position, unable to afford a good website and unsure of who to trust! With this in mind, I adapted my business approach to better serve small businesses. That's how I created 30% upfront and 14 monthly paymments which is manageable and affordable for businesses and their finances.

Bringing creativity and technical expertise to every project, my goal is to create beautiful, great performing websites while providing a smooth and enjoyable customer experience. Whether you're looking to launch a new website or give your site a fresh new look, I am eager to imagine new ideas and designs for your business's digital experience!
